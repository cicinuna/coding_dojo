PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS

This is a survival game designed for users to find weapons, avoid harmful gas-filled areas that surround more ares of the map as the game progresses, and eventually be the last-man-standing in order to win the game.
A player is matched against 99 other players, where each player freely drops on any part of the game map within the allowed flight trajectory.
Not only does one need to be masterful in aiming, one also needs instinctive, yet well-planned actions for choosing which part of the map to conquer or
which part of the map to wait in order to ambush other players. One can choose a variety of weapons ranging from melee weapons such as machete to powerful sniper rifles
such as the awm. In the table below, you can find a variety of weapons one can find in the game.
